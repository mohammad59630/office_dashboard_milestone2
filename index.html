Introduction

This page contains data from the the purpose of create the office database Dashboard for over 1000 different jobTitles.

The data visualizations that follow give a high-level overview of the data and clicking on the individual charts allows you to filter the results.

Once you have made your selections, you can look at the dropdown below to see who you have left.

Or maybe you just want to view the stats of different jobTitle? Choose one below and the page will display information for just that jobtitle.
